WHAT WE OFFER

Kids Sports

In addition to fostering athletic development, LittleAce helps combat the negative effects of excessive screen time by offering engaging and active alternatives. By encouraging children to spend more time in physical activities, LittleAce naturally reduces sedentary behaviour, promoting a balanced and healthy lifestyle. The organization aims to create well-rounded individuals by focusing on both fitness and character development.

Reducing screen time for children can have a wide range of positive effects on their physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Here are some key outcomes that can occur when kids spend less time on screens:

  1. Improved physical health
  2. Better Sleep Quality
  3. Enhanced Social Skills
  4. Increased Cognitive Development
  5. Better Mental Health
  6. Fostering Lifelong Habits
