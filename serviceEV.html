WHAT WE OFFER

Event Management

LittleAce can simplify and optimize many aspects of event management, from planning and communication to execution and post-event analysis. By providing a centralized platform that integrates scheduling, attendee management, vendor coordination, marketing, and more, LittleAce allows event organizers to focus on creating memorable experiences for their guests while reducing complexity and workload involved in event management.

Event Planning & Scheduling
Centralized Scheduling: LittleAce can help event planners set up a clear schedule for all event activities, such as guest arrivals, sessions, speakers, entertainment, and breaks. It ensures everything is time-bound and prevents scheduling conflicts.
Resource Allocation: Organizers can plan how resources like rooms, equipment, or personnel are assigned, helping to avoid double bookings and resource shortages.
Guest Management
Invitations and RSVPs: LittleAce can send digital invitations to attendees, track RSVPs, and manage guest lists, making it easier for organizers to monitor attendance and follow up with invitees.
Personalized Communication: The platform can automate personalized emails, reminders, or notifications, keeping guests informed about event details (location, time, dress code, etc.).
Event Collaboration
Team Collaboration Tools:Event management often involves multiple teams (marketing, logistics, customer service, etc.). LittleAce can provide collaboration tools that allow seamless communication and task assignment, ensuring that everyone is on the same page. Shared Dashboards: LittleAce can offer real-time dashboards where different teams can monitor the event’s progress, from attendee registration to vendor delivery and speaker management.
Venue & Vendor Management
Vendor Coordination: LittleAce can manage communications with vendors, from catering and AV setups to decor and transportation. Organizers can keep track of vendor contracts, payments, and timelines. Venue Booking: It can assist in booking venues, managing contracts, and ensuring that the venue is equipped with everything needed for a smooth event (e.g., tech setups, seating arrangements).
